Platform

About Our Team

The LED Grant Technology Team, funded by Louisiana Economic Development (LED), is committed to helping small businesses and entrepreneurs in Northeast Louisiana thrive through innovative technology solutions. We offer services such as website development, mobile app creation, and digital marketing support, all at no cost to local businesses. Our goal is to empower these businesses with the digital tools and expertise they need to enhance their online presence, streamline operations, and drive growth in a competitive market.
